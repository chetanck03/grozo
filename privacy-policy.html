Welcome to Grozo! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our smart grocery shopping app.

1. Information We Collect

Grozo collects and processes the following information to provide you with the best grocery shopping experience:

  • Grocery Lists: Items you add to your shopping lists, including names, quantities, and categories
  • Meal Plans: Meal preferences and plans you create using our AI-powered meal planner
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve functionality and user experience
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app optimization and performance

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized grocery shopping and meal planning features
  • Generate AI-powered meal suggestions and grocery recommendations using advanced AI models
  • Improve app performance, functionality, and user experience
  • Enable data synchronization across your devices (when applicable)
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

3. Data Storage and Security

Your personal data is primarily stored locally on your device. When you use our AI-powered meal planning features, your meal preferences may be securely transmitted to our AI service providers for processing. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

Grozo integrates with third-party AI services to provide AI-powered meal planning suggestions. When you use these features, your meal preferences and dietary requirements may be sent to our AI service providers for processing. We work only with reputable AI service providers who maintain high standards of data protection and privacy.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With service providers who assist us in operating the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app through the interface
  • Export: Use the "Export Lists" feature to download your data
  • Delete: Use the "Clear All Data" feature to permanently remove all your information
  • Control: Manage what information you share with AI services
  • Contact: Reach out to us with any privacy concerns or requests

7. Children's Privacy

Grozo is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

If you are using Grozo outside of your country of residence, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our service providers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and as described in this privacy policy. You can delete your data at any time using the app's built-in features.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date above.
